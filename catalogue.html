Order Process

Selection

  • Select a Plant, Pot and a design from our catalogue below.

  • Each item has a unique name & code. Note it down for the next step.

    • Design is optional. You May choose upto 2 designs per pot.

Order

  • Send in your selected item codes using the form below.

  • Alternatively, you may WhatsApp or call us directly on the numbers mentioned at the bottom of the page.

Confirmation

  • We'll contact you to confirm the order and process the payment.

  • We'll discuss any customisations and the delivery details of your order.

    • Google Pay, UPI, Paytm and direct bank transfers are accepted.

Delivery

  • As each order is handcrafted, we take a minimum of 10 days to process it.

  • Delivery or Pickup shall be arranged as decided during the confirmation call.
